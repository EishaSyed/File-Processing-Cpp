Ramadan, the ninth month of the Islamic calendar, is a period of fasting, prayer, reflection, and community for Muslims worldwide. It commemorates the revelation of the Quran and is one of the five pillars of Islam. During Ramadan, Muslims abstain from food and drink from dawn to sunset, engaging in increased worship, charity, and good deeds. It's a time for spiritual rejuvenation, self-discipline, and increased devotion to God
